About me

I am Dumebi (Du-may-be) Nnadirinwa Iwuchukwu, a designer based in Lagos, Nigeria. I have been working in design for over 9 years now. Presently I'm an Art Director at Webcoupers Consulting.


My work is progressive and human-centered with a focus on art direction and product design. I’m also a dreamer, an early-adopter and a chess player.


During my design journey, I've had the privilege of creating enabling experiences for people with brands like Budweiser, Nivea, Sprite, Schweppes, Sterling Bank, Globacom and more.


Teaching and mentorship has always been a huge part of my growth as a designer and you can book a free mentorship session with me on ADPlist.org for career advice, interview techniques, design leadership and portfolio reviews.
