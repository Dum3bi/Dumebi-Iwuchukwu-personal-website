Bio.

Dumebi Iwuchukwu is driven to develop solutions accessible to the bottom of the pyramid, fusing technology, design and culture in the areas of food, health, education and water supply.
He is a Designer, Web Developer, Brand Strategist and most of all a Teacher.
Dumebi is presently the Art Manager and Art Direction Instructor at Oxygen Academy Lagos where he teaches graphic design and art direction.
He is at his best when teaching, designing, coding and executing creative ideas.
He is a passionate practitioner of design thinking and believes the best solutions are found at the center of culture, design and technology.
Some of his favourite projects includes his work for O2 Academy Lagos, Dentiti Apparels, Lagos Live Theatre, Dangote Flour Mills, Breaking the Coconut Africa and Taruwa Festival 2019.
