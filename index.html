About

Dumebi

Art Director | Stategist | Teacher.

I am an Art Director and Brand Strategist currently teaching Art Direction and Digital Product Design at Oxygen Academy Lagos

I love to create enjoyable experiences for people. My medical radiography background helps me approach projects like i would attend to a patient, diagnosing problems before proferring solutions. I love nuclear physics, talk a lot (among friends) and play lots of first-person shooter games. I also design (Brand Identity, UX/UI), code (Web apps) and ask a lot of questions.

I am a member of the Interaction Design Foundation, the world’s largest UX Design learning community.
