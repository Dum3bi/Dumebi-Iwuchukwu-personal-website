More About Me

Art Director, Brand Strategist & Developer.

Howdy!

I believe in the Zulu philosophy of Ubuntu; I am because we are. This implies we are only truly human when we live for others in our community.

I feel the best solutions are designed in collaboration with the tribes that needs it, this makes the solutions Relevant, Original and Impactful, ensuring a High ROI for the brands involved.

I am an Art Manager and Resident Instructor (Art Direction) at Oxygen Academy, Lagos Nigeria.
I am also a trained Medical Radiographer and Sonographer with a bachelor's degree in Radiography and Radiological Sciences from Nnamdi Azikiwe University Awka, Nigeria.

I am driven to restore hope in Africa through capacity building, social entreprises and impact investing.
