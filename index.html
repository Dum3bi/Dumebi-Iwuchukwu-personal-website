About

Dumebi Iwuchukwu

Art Director & Product Designer

Dumebi's passion for experience design can be traced back to his formal training as a medical radiographer, where he spent significant time working on patient diagnosis and care.

As a designer, he specializes in art direction, brand identity, and product design.

He's had the privilege of creating enabling experiences for people with brands like Budweiser, Nivea, Sprite, Schweppes, Sterling Bank, Haptics, Globacom and more.
