Bio.

Dumebi Iwuchukwu is an Art Director, Web Developer, Brand Strategist and most of all a Teacher.
Dumebi is an Art Manager and Art Direction instructor at Oxygen Academy Lagos
He is at his best when teaching, designing, coding and executing creative ideas.
He is a passionate practitioner of design thinking and believes the best solutions are found at the center of culture, design and technology.
I am presently an Art Direction Instructor at Oxygen Academy Lagos
Some of my favourite projects involved my work for O2 Academy Lagos, Dentiti Apparels, Lagos Live Theatre, Dangote Flour Mills, Breaking the Coconut Africa and Taruwa Festival 2019.
My dream project is to travel the world building vernacular solutions with local communities, fusing technology, design and culture in the areas of food, health and water supply.
