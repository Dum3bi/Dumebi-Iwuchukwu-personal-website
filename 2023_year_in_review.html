2023 Year in Review

This format was inspired by David Hoang's Year in Review as it suited the minimalist style I wanted to adopt for my review..


Highs

  • Joined Stears as Design Lead in the beginning of the year
  • Was accepted into the Design Leadership Forum by InVision; a private global community of senior design leaders that share best practices, strategies, and resources across a range of design and leadership topics
  • Was promoted to Head of Design at Stears by May, 2023
  • Adenike and I celebrated five years together as a couple and one year married
  • Spoke at a conference and a couple of events and twitter spaces this year
  • Held 144 mentorship sessions for free on ADPList
  • Was recognised five times by ADPList as a top 1% Mentor on ADPList (February, March, July November & December)
  • Graduated from Dribbble's Design System Course by Dan Mall. Learnt so much about design system workflows, tools, documentation and governance.
  • Got a Free 5G Broadband Router from MTN
  • Spent quality time with my family
  • Held zero debts this year
  • Grew my savings this year

Lows

  • Wasn't consistent with exercising and didn't gain as much muscle as I planned
  • Wasn't consistent with praying and studying the bible daily
  • The 2023 elections cast a long shadow of violence and hate. Witnessing it online and at the polls left me feeling deeply hurt and disillusioned.

Professional

  • Produced a Data Visualisation Guideline for my team
  • Led a team of Senior Designers to deliver incredible work for Stears and our clients
  • Designed new product features for our Web Platform and Mobile App
  • Designed and built new Landing Pages and Data Products (Using Figma and Webflow)
  • Got better at Project Management and QA
