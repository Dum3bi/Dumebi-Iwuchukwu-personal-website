World Class Business by Design [Part 1]

  • June 21, 2018
  • By Dumebi Iwuchukwu

Animals are very interesting and exhibit some behaviours that deserve our attention. They migrate, fight, swim, mate, hunt, eat and drink, driven by their instinct to avoid unfavorable stimuli, organize for shelter and reproduce young ones of the species.

We are different in that though we do these things well, we also have the ability to create. This is a function of the gift of our imagination. With this we are able to create solutions mentally and then physically.

Before I go further, let me take us to the essence of this article. What is a business?

A business as a system that exists to create, deliver and capture value. It creates value by solving problems, meeting needs and enabling tasks to be accomplished. It delivers value through channels convenient to the end users and captures value through referrals, goodwill and revenue.
A world class business is simply a business that creates and captures value on a global scale.

The people who need design ingenuity the most, the poorest 90% of the global population, have historically been deprived of it.
Alice Rawsthorn, Design critic.

It might interest you to know that no global brand is an accident or a fluke. They were designed, planned and built.

In this series we will be exploring the ingredients, principles and strategies required to build a world class business. If you had looked closely at my definition you may have discovered my first ingredient. A world class business creates value, in other words, solves problems.
