SCRAPBOOK

My conscious appreciation of design and art started at the age of eight when my Mom gave me the gift of a scrapbook, Its wide empty pages filled my imagination with possibilities and there begun my journey as a designer.


I could say that's how my journey as a designer began. I would cut out comic strips and beautiful ads from my Dad's old magazines and newspapers


This scrapbook is not much different from the scrapbook of my childhood, as I'm still collecting images I love. It's just no longer from my dad's magazines but snippets from large projects I worked on, my photography or simply just experiments from the past 10 years.
