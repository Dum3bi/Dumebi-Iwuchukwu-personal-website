About

Dumebi (Du-may-be) Nnadirinwa Iwuchukwu is the Head of Design at Big Cabal Media where he leads a team of Brand and Product designers.


He has been working in design for about 10 years now with experience in Brand, Product and Marketing Design.


During his design journey, he've had the privilege of creating enabling experiences for people with brands like Google, Budweiser, Nivea, Sprite, Schweppes, Sterling Bank, Globacom and more.


Teaching and mentorship has always been a huge part of his career as a designer and you can book a free mentorship session with him on ADPlist.org for career advice, interview techniques, design leadership and portfolio reviews.
